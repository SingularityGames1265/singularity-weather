Stratus clouds

What are stratus clouds?

Stratus clouds are typically associated with a cloudy day. These can stretch for large horizontal distances and cover up the sky. The word stratus means layer in Latin

What other forms of stratus clouds are there?

You have two other kinds of stratus clouds; altostratus and cirrostratus. Altostratus clouds appear higher in the sky, although this is hard to see as the cloud covers up a large portion of it.

Cirrostratus clouds are more thin, usually present at the top of the troposphere. Light can come through more easily making the sky appear brighter.
