Region: Symbolica

Symbolica can be split up into three main areas.

The first consists of identically sized rooms. Each room is painted a different colour, like red, blue or green. Rooms hold no more than three pieces of random furniture, like chairs, tables, cabinets or rugs. These can be made of any material. The rooms also have atleast one white door. If a person closes the door they entered, the room they came from will be swapped for a different one. This makes getting lost a common practice. Some rooms also contain stairs ascending or descending to other floors. Doors can not only function as a way between other rooms, but also as a way to enter the second area, or rarely, as gateways.

The second area is made up of white concrete pillars stretching upwards and downwards. These pillars are connected with support beams of the same material. Rarely, a ceiling or floor is spun across these. There is reason to believe that this region holds unfinished rooms, as interior can be found on these floors and ceilings as well.

The pillars in this region stretch into the third region: a large black abyss. When listening closely, what can be best described as whale calls, can be heard originating from here. But no direct source from this noise has been discovered yet.
