About: References and Inspiration

The Void Plane gets quite a bit of inspiration from media. Many of sources of inspiration also focus heavily on the liminal space aesthetic.

To start off with the backrooms. The backrooms are amongst the most well known of this list. The original premise was that someone could clip out of reality by accident and land inside of the backrooms. They are a large liminal labyrinth, where no soul could be found. The only horror present used to be the sheer loneliness. But in modern times, they have multiple levels to them and can hold hostile entities looking for visitors of this domain.

Secondly, there is the Zooliminology Project. The Zooliminology Project is created by Quiietjay. It gives a wholesome twist to the otherwise frightening aestetic of liminal spaces. Instead of monsters, the liminal spaces hold passive creatures of interesting and sometimes adorable designs.

As a third, there is the Void series by the creator Lovvec. This takes a more Lovecraftian approach to the void aestetic. The Void in this series functions as a sort of liminal prison for many entities and its creator.
