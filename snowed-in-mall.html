Region: Dreaming We Were Snow

This region resembles a suburban neighborhood. that has been covered in snow. Apart from houses, it can also have public facilities like shops, parks and libraries. The architecture varies depending on the first person to enter the region. If the visitor was born in the United Kingdom, the region will mimic its architecture, featuring semi-seperated houses with white and red bricks as an example. The houses also hold interiors found on earth.

Like the houses, the facilities are all void of any life, not taking plants into account. Yet, the shelves of supermarkets seem to be stocked regularly. The products also variate depending on where the area "takes inspiration" from. The stores hold products that the visitor has used or eaten in their life, like food and toys. Some stores hold a section for premade meals. These meals have been noted to taste exactly the same, as meals that the visitor has eaten in their life, like a casserole their mother made for them as a child. Products that hold text like product packaging or books have the Babel Effect put on them.

This region has a weather cycle. The only preticipation is snow. It can be windy and incredibly foggy, allowing clear vision up to about 30 meters. The temperature is always below freezing, but inside the houses it differs between 16 and 20 degrees Celcius. Even though snow is common and does not melt, it never accumulates above 20 centimeters.
