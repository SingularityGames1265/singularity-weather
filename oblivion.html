Region: Adrenaline

Adrenaline is one of the more linear regions of the Void Plane. Its main feature is a highway brightly lit by sodium streetlights. On one side a large canal can be noticed. This canal contains regular water. On the other side of the canal, a large sprawling cityscape is seen, illuminated by its many colourful lights. Some of these lights come from the windows of the many buildings. Other lights are from the city's streets. There also seem to be many advertising signs scattered throughout. These are almost always illegible, as they have the Babel Effect put on them.

On the other side of the highway more water is present. It is not clear how far this streches onward, as it is quickly enveloped in a thick fog. Weirdly, cars can be found on the highway. These look similar to the cars on earth, but do not run on any fuel. They also have no lock on them, making them ideal for traversing the region. The cars can sometimes have a radio present in their interior. Turning this radio on plays either static noise or electronic music.

The region is entered and exited via highway tunnels, which unlike most aspects of Adrenaline, are completely unlit. These can be walked or driven into. If a person exits the region with a car, they will simply wake up in a new region or threshold without it.
