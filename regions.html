Flora and Fauna

This page is for all the flora and fauna found in the Void Plane.

Some regions in the Void Plane can contain flora found on earth, like grass and trees. When examined under the microscope, these plants appear genetically indifferent from their earthly counterparts. What is odd is that these plants can photosynthesise, even when no source of light is present.

The Void Plane does contain it's own unique flora and fauna. But unlike plants, no animals from earth have ever been spotted in the Void Plane.
