Entity: Mr. Sun

Mr. Sun is the only entity found in the region the Great Sea of the Unconcious. Mr. Sun is a tall and lanky entity with a humanoid appearance. Its total height is unknown, as it likes to crouch down to eye level. The entity is skeletal in form, having the outlines of its bones visible through its skin. The skin is fully black in colour and has the texture dried leaves.

Where a head would be expected to sit, a kind of sun is attached to the neck. This does not look like an actual star, but rather like a vintage sun. The face is perfectly round, with a constant smile. Mr. Sun does not blink, but can move its eyes. Its face has a subtle glow to it and feels similar to porselin.

It is curious in nature, usually checking out visitors of the region. It also appears to like staring at the horizon whilst sitting on one of the islands. Seeing it stand up straight is rare, as it slouches down when someone is nearby.
