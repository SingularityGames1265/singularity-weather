Gateways

Gateways are the main way to travel from region to region. They come in many different forms, but are usually seen as doors or windows. When opened, they are a direct portal to other regions. The region they open up to is always random. Closing a gateway, like shutting its door to then reopen it, could open it up to a different region than the one before. Most gateways differ in appearance, but are most commonly formed as a white wooden door complete with its frame.
