Regions

This page is for all the regions found in the Void Plane.

Regions are large areas that can be explored in multiple directions. Many of these regions appear to mimic human architecture, but lack any indication of being human-made. What created these places is unknown.

A leading theory is that pieces of earth glitched out of reality, taking on different shapes and sizes. This however, does not explain the unfamiliar features some regions hold, like flora and fauna or the Void Plane's non-euclidian nature.
