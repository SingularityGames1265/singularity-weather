Region: Great Sea of the Unconcious

The Great Sea of the Unconcious can be described as a large open water of some sorts, with a few sandy islands scattered throughout it. The fluid that makes up the sea is entirely black. Whilst it does have waves that crash like water on the shores, the fluid works more closely to a non-Newtonian fluid. If something slowly lowers into it, it would behave like water. But if it is hit with force, the fluid will act like a solid surface. If done with precision, one can run over the water as though it is a solid floor.

The islands are also quite the sight to behold. In direct opposition to the open water, the beaches seem to be lit by an unseen lightsource. Standing on an island, it would be about as bright as if it were a clear full moon. These islands can also be the home of the entity Mr. Sun. The sky has a dark grey hue to it, appearing lighter near the horizon. There are no stars or other objects in the sky present.

There are two different ways to exit this region. The first is to become fully submerged into the fluid. The second is to find a large door made of wood standing on one of the islands. This door is seemingly held up by just its frame. Digging beneath it, only reveals sand. If the surface under it becomes unable to support it properly, the door will disappear.
