Flora: Plaque

Plaque is a vine-like plant found in the region Oblivion. Like the rest of this region, it appears dark grey. The plant got its name from it's ways of growing. It lays unmoving, unless it is damaged. When a part of the plant is damaged, it will regrow in its entirety within seconds. Plaque can be found growing inside and on the walls or foundation of all buildings in its respective region. Working as a sort of rebar, it seems to be the only thing holding the buildings together.

A few seconds before Plaque regenerates, a small amount of liquid is secreted. Rubbing this on a wound or other form of damaged organic tissue, repairs it immediatly.
